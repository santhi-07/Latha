1. Time Management:

* Create a Schedule - Develop a realistic study schedule well in advance of the exam. Allocate specific time slots for each topic or subject.

* Prioritize Tasks - Focus on areas of weakness during your revision. Allocate more time to challenging topics.

* Use a To-Do List - Maintain a daily to do list to keep track of tasks. Cross off completed items for a sense of accomplishment.

2. Active Learning:

* Practice with Past Papers -Familiarize yourself with the exam format by practicing with past papers.

* Create Flashcards - Condense key information into flashcards for quick revie

* Teach the Material - Teach concepts to a study partner or imaginary audience. Explaining topics helps reinforce your understanding.

3. Stress Management:

* Practice Relaxation Techniques - Practice deep breathing or mindfulness to manage exam anxiety. Visualization techniques can also be helpful.

* Positive Self-Talk - Replace negative thoughts with positive affirmations. Remind yourself of your preparation and capabilities.
